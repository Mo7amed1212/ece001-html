Volume:

Volume refers to the sheer size of the ever-exploding data of the computing world. It raises the question about the quantity of data.

Velocity:

Velocity refers to the processing speed. It raises the question of at what speed the data is processed.

Variety:

Variety refers to the types of data. It raises the question of how disparate the data formats are

Veracity:

Veracity basically means the degree of reliability that the data has to offer.
